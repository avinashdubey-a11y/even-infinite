Frequently asked questions
What is Even Infinite, and how is it different from my health insurance?

Think of it as a layer on top of your existing insurance. Your policy stays untouched. Even Infinite does not alter the terms and conditions stated in your policy at all.

Insurance kicks in when something big happens — surgery, hospitalisation. Even Infinite is for everything before that. The routine GP visit. The blood test you've been putting off. The physio session. The maternity scan that arrives before any waiting period is done.

Upload your insurance certificate once, and you and your family get free outpatient care at Even Hospitals in Bengaluru. Plus 50% off any hospitalisation, surgery, or maternity from Day 1.

How is this different from wellness apps, OPD riders, or my company's existing health benefits?

Wellness apps give you articles and step counts. OPD wallets give you a limited annual budget — usually Rs 5,000 to Rs 25,000 — that usually runs out by June if your family actually uses it.

Even Infinite is a hospital program, not a wallet. No annual cap. No rationing. Real consultations, real diagnostics, real doctors. And 50% off inpatient care from the day you join.

Who is eligible?

Anyone in Bengaluru with active health insurance from one of our partner insurers. Group (through your employer) or retail, both are applicable. No age bar. Every family member on the policy can opt for Even Infinite.

Current partner insurers: Digit, ABHI, Manipal Cigna.

Coming soon: Star, Care, Narayana Health Insurance.

If your insurer is not on our partner list, join the waitlist and we'll let you know when your insurer is added.

I have multiple policies — corporate and retail. Which one should I upload?

All of them. The app accepts multiple documents in one go. We check each one independently, so more uploads means a better chance of activation. If some don't qualify, we activate against the ones that do and tell you clearly about the rest.

How do I sign up, and how long does activation take?

Three steps: name, mobile, pincode. Upload your Certificate of Insurance. Submit. That's the whole sign-up.

We do a manual review within 48 hours to confirm the policy is active. Once approved, benefits start immediately. If something's off, we tell you exactly why and you can re-upload. No limit on retries.

Are slots limited? How does the waitlist work?

Yes, for now, because we do not want to compromise on the quality of experience provided by Even Infinite.

Once slots fill up, sign-ups move to a waitlist. We open new batches every few weeks. You will receive an email when it's your turn.

Does this cover my whole family?

Yes. Everyone on your partner policy is covered under one activation. If a family member has their own separate policy, the app allows you to add them by uploading their policy document. Each person gets their own profile. Multiple family members can use the hospital facilities at the same time — no concurrency limit.

Can I activate the membership on behalf of my parents?

If they're on your policy, they're already included. If they have a separate policy from a partner insurer, you can add them through the app. If their insurer isn't with us yet, they'd need to be on a partner policy first before we can activate.

What's included with the membership?

You can avail the benefits of Even Infinite at Even Hospital, Race Course Road, Bengaluru exclusively. Our own hospital, our own team.

  • Free, unlimited consultations across specialities — General Physician, Internal Medicine, Paediatrics, Orthopaedics, General Surgery, Gastroenterology, ENT, Dermatology, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Cardiology (non-tertiary), Nephrology (non-tertiary), Psychiatry, plus Dietetics and Physiotherapy.
  • Free diagnostics (when prescribed) — full pathology, X-ray, ultrasound, CT, mammography, ECG, 2D Echo, TMT, EEG, Pap Smear
  • Free preventive screenings — protocol-led, prescribed by Even doctors based on your age, family history, and risk profile
  • Free dietician, physiotherapy, and mental-health (psychology) sessions
  • Free 24/7 teleconsultations with the Even Care doctor team — bookable from the app
  • 20% off on prescribed medicines at the Even Hospital pharmacy

There is no per-visit fee, no consultation charge, and no annual cap on the number of OPD or diagnostic services you can use.

What is NOT included?

If you can book it through Even, it's covered. We don't have a fixed exclusions list. As Even hospital's offerings expand, they will be extended to your membership.

If there's a service that an Even doctor recommends that Even hospital doesn't cater to, you will be informed and our care planners will assist you with the next step through your insurance at a tertiary partner facility.

For now, here is what's not included.

  • Outpatient: MRI scans, genetic testing, ophthalmology consultations, dental procedures
  • Inpatient: complex tertiary surgeries (organ transplants, cardiac surgery, neurosurgery), eye surgeries and procedures, cosmetic and fertility procedures, in-patient psychiatric hospitalisation
How does the 50% discount on hospitalisation, surgery, and maternity work?

At admission, you choose Even Infinite as your payment route. You get 50% off the full bill — room, doctor fees, OT, consumables, all of it. Nothing to file or claim back afterwards.

The discount applies to the whole admission. You can't split it with another payment route.

Maternity works the same way. Normal delivery, C-section, antenatal, postnatal, newborn screening. No maternity waiting period.

Is there a list of procedures eligible for the IPD discount? What about emergency hospitalisation?

No fixed list. If Even Hospital can do the procedure, the 50% applies — except the exclusions above.

For emergencies at Even Hospital, the membership works exactly like a planned admission. If you end up at another hospital because the situation required it, Even Infinite won't apply there. During an emergency, always go to the closest competent facility first.

I already need a surgery. Can I sign up just for that?

Yes. The discount kicks in as soon as your membership is active.

We're honest about this because we'd rather you know upfront. But the model is built for people who want a real care relationship over time, not a one-time discount. Members who consistently skip their quarterly check-ins won't have their membership renewed.

Do I need to see an Even doctor first to use any service?

No. Any valid prescription works — from your regular GP, a specialist, anyone. Upload it in the app and book directly. We'll honour it. A follow-up with an Even doctor is always worth it when possible. They know the pathways here and can connect the dots across your care. Second opinions are included.

How does Even Infinite affect my insurance premium, NCB, or claims?

Not at all. The two have nothing to do with each other. We file nothing with your insurer. Your premium, your NCB, your policy — all remain untouched.

If you use Even Infinite for an admission, the bill is settled at the counter. That's the end of it.

Where can I use it and how do I book?

Even Hospital on Racecourse Road, Bengaluru. OPD hours are between 9 a.m. to 7 p.m. on weekdays, with more slots being added. Current availability is live in the app.

Book everything through the app — consultations, diagnostics, teleconsults, follow-ups. Walk-ins are welcome too; the front desk can activate your membership on the spot. Just bring your phone.

Can I cancel? What happens if my insurance changes or expires?

Cancel any time. No fee, no notice period.

If your insurance lapses or you switch jobs, the membership pauses. Upload a new policy document when you're covered again and it reactivates — no fresh application needed. Missing a quarterly check-in does the same thing. One check-in and you're back.

How is my data handled?

Your records stay in Even's systems. You (through the app) along with the Even doctor, both have access to it.

We may use anonymised, aggregated data to improve the network. Nothing that ever identifies you.
