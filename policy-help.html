How to find your policy
document?

You need to upload a document showing your policy number. Here are the easiest places to find it.

Method 1

Search your email

Your insurer sent a policy PDF when you bought, renewed, or paid premium. Any of these has your policy number.

  1. Open your email inbox
  2. Search for your insurer's name: "Aditya Birla Health", "ManipalCigna", or "Digit"
  3. Open any email — welcome mail, premium receipt, renewal notice, or 80D certificate
  4. Download the attached PDF — upload it here
Also check Promotions and Spam folders.

Method 2

Insurer app or website

Log in with your registered mobile number to download a fresh copy of your policy.

  1. Open the app or website above
  2. Log in with your registered mobile number + OTP
  3. Go to "My Policies" → select your policy
  4. Tap "Download Policy" — save the PDF and upload it here

Method 1

Find your e-card

Your e-card has your policy number and member ID from a TPA when you're added to the policy.

Beneficiary name Member ID Date of birth Validity Policy holder TPA
  1. Open your inbox (check work email first — e-cards often go there)
  2. Search for "e-card", "health card", "mediclaim", "health insurance" or any TPA name: Medi Assist, MedBuddy, Vidal Health, Paramount, Health India
  3. Open the attached PDF or image
  4. Upload the e-card here

Method 2

Ask your HR

Your HR can share the e-card or the policy document directly.

  1. Email your HR or People Ops team
  2. Ask for your e-card or policy document
  3. They usually respond within one working day
  4. Save the file they share and upload it here

Message template

Hi team, could you please share my health insurance e-card or policy document for the group policy with [Insurer]? I need it to complete my Even Healthcare onboarding. Thanks!

Method 3

Download from the TPA portal

If the e-card email is lost, fetch it directly from the TPA.

  1. Ask your HR which TPA manages your policy (or check the sender of past insurance emails)
  2. Visit the TPA portal — common ones: medibuddy.in/ecard, mediassistindia.com, vidalhealthtpa.com
  3. Verify with your name, employee ID, or registered mobile + OTP
  4. Download your e-card — upload it here

Method 4

Try the insurer app

Some group policies show up in the insurer's own app too.

  1. Download your insurer's app (Activ Health / myManipalCigna / Digit)
  2. Log in with the mobile number registered by your employer
  3. Go to "My Policies"
  4. If your policy shows up, download the PDF and upload it here
